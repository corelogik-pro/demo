Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LIC policies

What is the minimum premium for LIC policies?
The minimum premium varies by policy, but many plans start as low as ₹100 per month. Child plans and term insurance typically have lower premiums, while investment-focused policies may require higher contributions.
How do I choose the right insurance plan?
The best policy depends on your financial goals, age, income, and family situation. During your free consultation, we'll assess your needs and recommend plans that provide optimal coverage and benefits for your specific situation.
What documents are required to buy a policy?
You'll need identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN), address proof, age proof, and income documents. For certain policies, a medical examination may be required. We guide you through the entire documentation process.
Can I get a loan against my LIC policy?
Yes, most traditional LIC policies allow you to take loans after 3 years of premium payment. The loan amount depends on the surrender value of your policy. We can help you understand the terms and process.
